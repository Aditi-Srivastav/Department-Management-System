About Us

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) at the Institute of Engineering and Rural Technology (IERT) aims to provide the best in computer science education. With cutting-edge curriculum and state-of-the-art facilities, we strive to nurture talent and encourage creativity among students. Our goal is to equip students with the skills required to thrive in a dynamic technological landscape and become leaders in the field of technology.

Our department offers a wide array of academic programs and research opportunities in various domains of computer science, including AI, data science, software engineering, and more.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a globally recognized center of excellence in computer science and engineering education. We aim to create a nurturing environment where students can explore their potential, innovate, and contribute to the advancement of technology. Through a comprehensive curriculum, world-class research opportunities, and collaboration with industry leaders, we strive to shape the future of technology and foster a culture of continuous learning and growth.
